Best Parks in Fulshear, TX

Fulshear, Texas offers beautiful parks and outdoor spaces, especially within its master-planned communities. Here's your complete guide.

Master- Planned Community Parks

Cross Creek Ranch Parks

Multiple parks throughout the community with playgrounds, trails, and picnic areas. The central park features a lazy river and resort-style pool.

Fulbrook Parks

Beautiful green spaces, walking trails, and fishing lakes. Community center with fitness facilities.

Westin Lakes Parks

Parks with playgrounds, sports fields, and scenic lake views.

Public Parks

  • Fulshear Park - Main public park with sports fields
  • Timber Lane Park - Neighborhood park with trails

Things to Do

Parks in Fulshear offer hiking trails, fishing, playgrounds, sports courts, and community events throughout the year.
